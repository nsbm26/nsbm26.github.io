Conference Scope

The seminar aims to promote scientific exchange and interdisciplinary collaboration in the field of bioactive molecules and their innovative biotechnological applications.

We welcome original research contributions related to, but not limited to, the following scientific themes.

Scientific Topics

🩺 Topic 1 — Pharmacology of Bioactive Molecules and Citizen Health

  • Drug discovery
  • Pharmacology
  • Toxicology
  • Therapeutic applications
  • Medicinal chemistry

🌱 Topic 2 — Bioactive Molecules for Food Security and Agro-industry

  • Functional foods
  • Food biotechnology
  • Natural preservatives
  • Agricultural biotechnology
  • Sustainable food production

🔬 Topic 3 — Microbial Biotechnologies and Energy Security

  • Industrial microbiology
  • Fermentation technologies
  • Biofuels
  • Renewable energy
  • Environmental microbiology

🌍 Topic 4 — Environmental Valorization and Preservation

  • Bioremediation
  • Circular bioeconomy
  • Waste valorization
  • Green technologies
  • Environmental biotechnology

🤖 Topic 5 — Digital Innovation and Artificial Intelligence in Biotechnology

  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Machine Learning
  • Bioinformatics
  • Computational Biology
  • Digital Biotechnology

Submission Guidelines

Authors are invited to submit an original abstract presenting unpublished research.

Submitted abstracts must

  • be written in English or French;
  • clearly state the objectives, methodology, results, and conclusions;
  • address one of the seminar themes;
  • present original work that has not been previously published.
